Badges

Badges are colored elements containing small bits of information. They are used for labelling content, displaying metadata and/or highlighting information. we can say that badges are used to highlight an item's status for quick recognition.

Types of Badges

IQRA UI includes three types of badges 1) badges on icon 2) badges on avatar and one more 3) badges on text. Badges are used in text like when some new news updated on website they use yellow color badge with new keyword and placed beside that updated news.
